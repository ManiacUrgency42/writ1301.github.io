# Sustainability for Formula 1's Long Logistics

## Thomas

Emissions Comparison: Air vs Sea Freight
<img width="959" height="749" alt="emissions_comparison" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/78859976-b6fa-4b63-8f0e-9d4b45947c70" />

Air freight produces much higher CO₂ emissions per tonne-kilometer than sea freight. Switching intercontinental logistics from planes to cleaner-fuel cargo ships could reduce emissions from these transport legs by 60–90%, depending on the fuel used.
